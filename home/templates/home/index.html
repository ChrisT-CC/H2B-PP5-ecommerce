About

We are a team of hobby beekeepers who love nature and our healthy honey bees. We have small apiaries in and around London.
All of our honey is raw, unpasterurised, extracted with minimal interference to the bees and it's not heated or treated.
Along with our honey, we have other honey bee healthy products, like infused honey, beeswax or candles and pollen with all of its natural benefits.
